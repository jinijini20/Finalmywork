Getting Lat/Lng from a Click Event

This example listens for the click event, gets the latitude and longitude coordinates
of the click from google.maps.MapMouseEvent.latLng, and displays those coordinates in
an info window.

Read the documentation [https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/javascript/events#EventProperties].
